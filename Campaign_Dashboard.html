✅ Paid Push Consistent Winner

Both apps show positive lift on paid user push campaigns. SU Plays Like hit 98.4% confidence at +2.77pp — strongest result in program. Pattern is reliable.

⚠️ Free Push Mixed Results

Free user push underperforms or matches control in both apps. SU Plays Like -0.28pp; GS AST -0.10pp. Likely segment-level effect — free users are more passive.

🔴 SU Email 0-for-3

Three feature highlight weeks, zero meaningful email lift in SU. May reflect list quality, subject line weakness, or email as wrong channel for this audience. Flag for Jacob.

📊 Sub Starts Signal

Shot Tracking iOS push drove 106 sub starts (treatment) vs 7 (control) despite only 69.65% confidence. Strong monetization signal worth tracking even below session confidence threshold.
